Privacy Policy

How we handle your data at Brewmie

Your shots stay on your device — unless you sign in.

Without signing in, everything lives in your browser only. We can't see it. Clear your browser data to delete it.

If you sign in, we sync your shots, gear setup, and preferences across your devices.

What we collect.

Signed-in: your email, display name, shots, gear, and settings — enough to sync them.

Anonymous shot data — grind, yield, time, taste — feeds the recommendation engine. No names, no emails, no device IDs. Turn it off any time in Setup → Privacy.

Sign-in.

Optional. We get your account ID and display name. We never see your password.

Cookies.

For sign-in sessions, preferences, and anonymous analytics. Users in the EU/UK are asked on first visit.

Your rights.

Access, correct, export, or delete your data any time. The app has a Delete all data button in Insights. To delete a signed-in account, email us.

Children.

Not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect their data.

Contact.

Questions, feedback, deletion requests? chef@lazysous.app
