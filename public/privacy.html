Privacy Policy

How we handle your data at Brewmie

Your shots stay on your device — unless you sign in.

Without signing in, your shots, gear setup, and preferences live in your browser only. We can't see them. Clear your browser data to delete everything.

When you sign in, we store your data on secure servers (Supabase) so you can access it from any device.

What we collect and why.

If you sign in, we store your account details (email, display name), shots, gear setup (machine, grinder, beans), maintenance dates, and app settings. This lets everything sync across your devices.

We also collect anonymous, aggregated shot data — grind size, yield, time, taste rating — to improve the dialling-in recommendations Brewmie gives every user. You can turn this off any time in Setup → Privacy.

Who sees your data.

Only you can see your account-linked shots and gear setup. We use Supabase to host and sync your data — they help us run the app but don't use your data for their own purposes.

How long we keep it.

Synced data is stored until you delete your account or ask us to remove it. Anonymous usage analytics are kept for up to 90 days.

Your rights.

At any time, you can access, correct, delete, or export your data — or contact us for help. You can manage your data in the app's Setup screen.

Cookies & tracking.

We use cookies to manage sign-in sessions, store preferences, and capture anonymous usage stats. Users in the EU/UK are asked to consent on first visit.

Subscriptions.

Brewmie offers optional auto-renewing Premium subscriptions. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel anytime in your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Authentication.

We support Sign in with Apple and Google Sign-In. We receive only your account identifier and display name — never your password. You can sign out or delete your account at any time.

Children.

Brewmie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Security.

Data transfers are encrypted. Sign-in is protected. Local storage is sandboxed in your browser. No payment info is stored by us — payments are handled entirely by Apple.

International transfers.

Our servers may be hosted in the US or EU. We rely on Supabase's infrastructure and data protection standards.

Contact.

Questions, feedback or deletion requests? Email us anytime at barista@brewmie.app.
