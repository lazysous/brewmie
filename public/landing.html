What is Brewmie?

Brewmie is an espresso dial-in coach for home baristas. You set up your kit once (espresso machine, grinder, beans), pull a shot, tell Brewmie how it tasted, and the app tells you exactly what to change for the next shot. Grind finer by half a notch on your grinder. Drop the dose by 0.3 g. Pull 2 seconds longer. Reasoning underneath every move, in plain language.

How dial-in works in Brewmie

Espresso dial-in is the loop every home barista runs: pull, taste, adjust, pull again, taste, adjust, until the cup is balanced. The hard part isn't the loop. It's knowing which variable to move and how far. Brewmie watches the four signals that matter (shot time, yield ratio, taste descriptors, bean age) and converts them into one specific dial change at a time.

Behind the recommendation: a roast-aware starting model, same-bag memory so the second shot off a new bag isn't a guess, a gusher guard for the channelling cases, a crema bias, and a cold-machine warmup adjustment. The model is honest about uncertainty. When the data is thin (first shot off an unknown bag), Brewmie says so and offers a safe starting point, not a confident wrong answer.

Designed to be light

Brewmie is the app you want when you're standing at the machine in pyjamas before work and the milk is steaming. Two taps to log. One screen to rate. One number to dial. No essays, no calorie counts, no fitness gamification. Just espresso.

Pricing

Free to download and use. Brewmie Premium is a one-off purchase (around AUD 6.99, localised by the App Store and Google Play in your region). Premium unlocks per-grinder dial units, lifetime shot history, personal benchmarks, and CSV export. There is no subscription. There never will be. Buy it once, it's yours.

Privacy

Brewmie has no advertising SDKs, no data brokers, no tracking. The optional sign-in (Apple or Google) is used to sync your premium unlock and shot history across your own devices. Anonymous product analytics can be disabled in Setup › Privacy. Account deletion is one tap from the profile menu. Your shots, your gear, and your sign-in are hard-deleted immediately.
