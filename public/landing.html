How dialling in works.

Three to six shots to a recipe you can repeat every morning.

i.

Set your kit.

Pick your machine, grinder, beans. Five minutes, done. Brewmie remembers everything.

ii.

Pull a shot.

Usual grind, 18g dose, 36g out. Tap Brew, log the time and yield, rate the taste.

iii.

Read the call.

One line of plain English. Go finer 0.5. Add 0.3g. Hold. The reason sits underneath.

iv.

Apply and repeat.

Hit Apply, dial in the new setting, pull again. By shot four or five you're locked in for the bag.
